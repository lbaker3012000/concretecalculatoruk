Frequently Asked Questions
About

This free concrete calculator was built to help UK homeowners, DIYers, and tradespeople quickly estimate materials for concrete pours. Quantities are based on standard UK mix ratios and typical builders merchant bag sizes.

How to Use

Select your mix type, enter your dimensions in metres, millimetres, feet, or inches, adjust the waste allowance, and click Calculate. Results include tonbag and 20kg bag quantities, estimated costs, and links to UK suppliers.
