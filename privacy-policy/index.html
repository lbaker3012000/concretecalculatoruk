Privacy Policy

// Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to Concrete Calculator UK, accessible at concretecalculatoruk.co.uk. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

2. Information We Collect

This website is a free tool and does not require you to create an account or submit any personal information to use the calculator. We do not collect names, email addresses, or payment details.

We may collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics and Google AdSense, including pages visited, time on site, browser type, and approximate geographic location. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. Cookies

This site may use cookies placed by third-party services including Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These cookies help us understand how visitors use the site and allow Google to serve relevant advertisements. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this site. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Third-Party Links

This website contains links to third-party websites including builders merchants and suppliers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites and encourage you to read their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

7. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Any anonymous analytics data is retained by Google in accordance with their own data retention policies.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase any personal data we hold about you. As we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data to access or delete. For queries about third-party data (Google Analytics, AdSense), please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact information available on this website.
